Class 10 Subjects
Mathematics

Explore advanced concepts in algebra, geometry, trigonometry, and statistics. Solve problems related to quadratic equations, circles, and probability.

Science

Dive into topics like physics (motion, force, and energy), chemistry (chemical reactions and acids/bases), and biology (cell structure and human physiology).

History

Study ancient civilizations, medieval history, and modern world events. Learn about revolutions, wars, and influential leaders who shaped the world.

Geography

Understand the Earth's physical features, climate patterns, natural resources, and population dynamics. Explore maps and environmental issues.

English

Improve your reading, writing, and comprehension skills. Study grammar rules, literature, and creative writing techniques.

Bengali

Learn the rich language of Bengali through grammar, literature, and cultural studies. Explore famous works by renowned Bengali authors.
